## Origin and Naming System of Glock

Glock is a pistol series developed by Austrian engineer Gaston Glock in the 1980s, renowned worldwide for its innovative polymer frame design and reliability. For those new to Glock, you might be curious about its naming convention. [Why does Glock have G17, G18, G19 numbering?](/posts/glock-numbering-system) is a common question many people have.

## Generation Evolution History

Over time, Glock has undergone multiple technical improvements and upgrades, from the original first generation to today's fifth generation, each with its specific enhancements. To quickly understand the differences between generations, please refer to the [Glock Gen 1-5 Overview](/posts/glock-generations-overview).

## Diverse Caliber Options

Additionally, Glock offers various [caliber versions](/posts/glock-caliber-comparison), ranging from common 9mm, .40 S&W to .45 ACP, meeting the needs of different users. Whether for law enforcement, military purposes, or civilian shooting enthusiasts, everyone can find a suitable model.
